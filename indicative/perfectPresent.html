Indicative Mood: Perfect Present

Perfect Present (Haber: Have / Has)

Short Definition: For actions that just recently start and ended in the past.

Tips

  • Auxiliary Verb + Haber Conjugation + Past Participle
  • Because you are conjugating the Haber, the following verb will be unconjugated but in past participle form
  • Past participle form is akin to verb in past tense kind of like verbs ending in 'ed'
  • Auxiliary Verb + Haber Conjugation + Past Participle
