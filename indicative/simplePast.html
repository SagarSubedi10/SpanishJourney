Indicative Mood: Simple Past

Simple Past (Preterite)

Short Definition: This tense is used for referring to the past, when the action has a clear ending point. It happened in the past and finished in the past.

Tips

Conjugations

Verbs Hablar Comer Vivir
Yo Hable Comi Vivi
Tu Hablaste Comiste Viviste
El, Ella Hablo Comio Vivio
Nosotros Hablamos Comimos Vivimos
Ello, Ellas Hablaron Comieron Vivieron

Irregular

Examples

  • She worked at night - El trabajo en noche
  • I (talked/spoke) with Manuel yesterday - Hable con Manuel ayer
  • We watched Batman last week - Vimos Batman este semana pasado
  • I was at home for the whole day yesterday - Estuve en mi casa por todo el dia ayer
