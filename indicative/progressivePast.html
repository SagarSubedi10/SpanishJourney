Indicative Mood: Progressive Past

Progressive Past (was / were)

Short Definition: If something happens in the past while another action is taking place. Progressive past is for the ongoing action.

Tips

  • Subject + Imperfect Estar + Progressive Verb
  • I was studying when my friends invited me to go out

Examples
