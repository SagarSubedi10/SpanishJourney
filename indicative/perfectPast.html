Indicative Mood: Perfect Past

Perfect Past (had)

Short Definition: Used to discuss actions that took place prior or another completed action.

Tips

  • Subject + Haber conjugated in imperfect + action in its past participle
  • The concept of an action being completed before another previous action doesnt always need to be explicitly stated.
  • I had studied enough before taking the exam VS I had studied enough

Examples

  • This day has been a disaster - Ese dia habia sido un desastre
  • Maria no habia viajado en tren hasta ayer - Maria had no traveled by train until yesterday
  • I had enjoyed the concert a lot - Habia disfrutado mucho del concierto
  • Pero tu no habias usando algo asi antes - But you had never used something like that before
