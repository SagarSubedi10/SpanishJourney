Indicative Mood: Progressive Present

Progressive Present

Short Definition: We use progressive tenses to tell that something is happening at the moment, or that it was happening at the moment were describing. The use of progressive tenses is very common.

Tips

  • This sense is same as 'ING' verbs in english
