Indicative Mood: Simple Tense

Imperfect Past

Short Definition: Indicative imperfect past is used for past actions. Specifically for the past actions that done habitually, not completed in the past, and characteristics/emotional states that took place in the past. These actions kind of "float" in the past with no definitive ending.

Tips

Conjugations

Verbs Hablar Comer Vivir
Yo Hablaba Comia Vivia
Tu Hablabas Comias Vivias
El, Ella Hablaba Comia Vivia
Nosotros Hablabamos Comiamos Viviamos
Ello, Ellas Hablaban Comian Vivian

Irregular

Examples

  • Tu eras la ganadora - You used to be the winner
  • Me contaba cuentos sobre un ninez - He used to tell me stories when he was a kid
  • Ellos escribian libros - they used to write books (note this isnt the same as they WROTE a book)
  • El hablaba nepali cuando era pequena - think of it as: he used to speak nepali
