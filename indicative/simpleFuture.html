Indicative Mood: Simple Future

Simple Future

Short Definition: Actions that will take place in the future

Tips

  • English equivalent = "will + verb"
  • Note this concept: "(voy, vas, va, vamos, van) + a" Kind of an unoffical way to speak about the future using the present tense + conjugated ir to say "going to"
  • Note that the conjugations for all verbs, ER, AR, and IR follow the same structure

Conjugations

Verbs Hablar Comer Vivir
Yo Hablare Comere Vivire
Tu Hablaras Comeras Viviras
El, Ella Hablara Comera Vivira
Nosotros Hablaremos Comeremos Viviremos
Ello, Ellas Hablaran Comeran Viviran

Irregular

Examples

  • El resturante cerrara mas temprano - The resturant will close early
  • No encontraremos un mejor precio - We will not find a better price
  • We should go to the park now. It will rain at 5. - Deberiamos ir al parque ahora. Llovera mas tarde
  • If I buy you this, will you be happy? - Si te compro este, estaras feliz?
  • We are going to eat chicken and rice - vamos a comer pollo con arroz
  • Are you going to wait for me? - Me vas a esperar
